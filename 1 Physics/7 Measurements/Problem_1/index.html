Discussion on Uncertainties and Sources of Error Measurement resolution: The length uncertainty is very small due to fine ruler resolution; thus its contribution to uncertainty is minimal.

Timing variability: Human reaction time and stopwatch resolution dominate timing uncertainty. Taking average over 10 oscillations reduces error.
