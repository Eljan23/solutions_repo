🌊 Problem 1: Interference Patterns on a Water Surface from Point Sources at Regular Polygon Vertices ✨ Motivation Wave interference on a water surface visually demonstrates how coherent waves interact—leading to constructive interference (amplification) or destructive interference (cancellation). By arranging multiple point sources at the vertices of a regular polygon, we can explore symmetrical and complex interference patterns.

Such analysis helps us understand:

Wave superposition

Phase relationships

Applications in acoustics, optics, and antenna design
